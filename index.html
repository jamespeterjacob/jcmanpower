About Us

We take on erection jobs for major companies across the globe. Some of our ongoing work is at APOLLO Tyres India Pvt. Ltd. Extruders, mills, conveyors and Structural work (Approximately 2000 Ton) & Kone Elevators India Pvt. Ltd. (Kasto automatic sheet storage and retrieval system)

We perform all types of machinery erection & installation on Tonnage basis that include Mechanical, Rigging, Electrical & Turnkey Jobs. With vast industry knowledge we supply skilled manpower that include Engineers, Supervisors, Safety Officers, Store Keepers, Mildred Fitters, Fabricators, Tig Welders, Mechanical Fitters, Pipe Fitters, Arc Welders, Riggers, Mechanics, Asst. Mechanics, Electricians, Painters, Flagmen as well as experienced laborers.

In 2019 we successfully completed in excess of 200 projects.
experience
Heavy Equipment Operators: like Excavator Operators, Bulldozer Operators, Wheel Loader Operators, Crane Operators, Forklift Operators, Truck Drivers and Car Drivers.

We supply material required for Erection of Heavy Machineries, Supporting Materials like Steel Plate, Pipes, Sheets, Structural Beams, Angles, Cable Trays, and Electrical Fittings.

We also supply consumables like Paints, Cutting Wheels, Grinding Wheels, Buffing Wheels and all other Supporting Materials.

Wide Experience

We at JC MANPOWER SUPPLIES are in this business of machinery handling and erection work since two decades. We have earned this reputation by virtue of our Outstanding Tenure in this Industry and also by serving our Clients diligently, as per their needs and providing them the best quality service required in this business with no compromise on Punctuality and Performance.

Our team is proud to say that we have been together from the beginning and we stand committed to the highest performance standard, to execute tasks with accuracy and precision in time bound manner. Our focus is on providing excellent service to our clients with the highest standard of Safety and Reliabilitysss. fully complying with requirement of the quality management system and our esteemed clients require.

We have always followed the basics (Safety & Customer Service) for all around us in all the work we have completed in the past and in doing so we have always followed the Safety Rules & Regulations ensured by the company for the safety of all employees.
